# About Signal & Structure

Signal & Structure creates practical executive systems for planning, governance, prioritization, and decision support.

The goal is simple: help leaders and operators reduce chaos, improve clarity, and make better decisions with tools that are ready to use immediately.

## What Signal & Structure builds
- governance kits
- operator templates
- executive meeting tools
- planning and prioritization systems
- decision-support artifacts

## Who it's for
- executives
- IT leaders
- operators
- PMO teams
- consultants
- founders who need clearer systems and better execution
