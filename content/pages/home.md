# Signal & Structure

## Practical executive systems for planning, governance, and decision-making

Signal & Structure creates practical tools for leaders and operators who need clearer meetings, better prioritization, and stronger decision support.

## Why it exists
Too many templates are generic, over-designed, or detached from how real meetings and operating rhythms actually work. Signal & Structure focuses on practical tools that can be used in live business settings.
